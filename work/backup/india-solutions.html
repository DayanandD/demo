SIS Security Solutions India Nobody covers the Nation like us

Retina Security Services is a critical solutions market leader, providing a wide range of comprehensive technology-enabled security solutions across multiple categories across India.
With a presence in Asia-Pacific and over 40 years of industry experience, the organisation provides world-class service quality and compliance assurance across 630 districts in India. SIS Security provides world-class security solutions to 33,128 sites using unique and proprietary software.

The group offers security professionals for specialised guarding, technology-enabled Security Solutions, e-surveillance, system integration, alarm monitoring, and response services to a diverse variety of customers in the commercial and public sectors across the country.
Retina Security Services offers technology-enabled solutions that give our customers increased visibility, real-time accountability, greater control, and complete transparency.

We have successfully deployed numerous Tech solutions across 4240 sites for over 1100 customers in India, thanks to our extensive network of trusted OEMs, world-class technology partners, and joint ventures. Our interoperable AI-enabled video and IoT-enabled access providers contribute to the development of more customised solutions for our customers' demands.
