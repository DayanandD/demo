Training and Development in Security Solutions

Retina Security Services provides numerous training interventions to its security staff. The pedagogy is developed in accordance with the PSARA Act criteria, and it trains Security Guards, Rapid Response Teams, Security Supervisors, GTS, GTOs, and other personnel in 22 fully residential training academies scattered across India, each with cutting-edge training facilities.

Fresh hires go through a 28-day training programme at our in-house training institutes, where they learn skills related to their work and career development. All training syllabi have emphasised theory, practical, physical, and game-based learning.

In addition, Retina Security Services provides unique training for its Quick Response Team - QRT Officers. The training includes resolving low-tech encounters with intruders, removing false triggers, and dealing with medical emergencies.

After successfully completing training, all security personnel were assigned to different units within Retina Security Services Enterprises based on their rank/designation and job fit. Post-deployment, periodic training is provided on a regular basis through Retina Security Services's own training platform 'M-trainer', the first in the security business.

The mobile training platform, which is distributed across India via training vans, tablets, and mobile phones, allows for ongoing on-the-job training in areas like as fire safety, searching and frisking, mob control, access control, and vehicle management. M-Trainer learning modules are also offered according to the client's specifications.
